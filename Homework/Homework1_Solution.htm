Instructions for assessment

Full score requires the correct answer as well as an explanation/derivation. There is a total of 80 points. 

1. A lawnmower with four standard wheels has only one degree of freedom moving on the plane and two degrees of freedom when considering "pitch" (by pressing down the handle). Either answer "one" or "two" is correct with explanation (5 points). The lawnmower cannot turn, unless the front wheels are lifted off the ground by pushing down on its rear handle. This removes one kinematic constraint, allowing the lawnmower to move forwards and backwards (1 DOF) as well as to pivot about the center point of its rear axle. (5 points)

2. There are three degrees of freedom on the XY-plane, forward/backward, sideways, and rotation around the z-axis (5 points).

3. a) The angle between two vectors is given by the "dot product". The dot product is the sum of the products of each individual entries. In the example provided, these products add up to zero. In this case, the two vectors are orthogonal. (You can verify this by solving cos(angle)=0.)  (10 points)

b) In order to form a coordinate system, all vectors need to be orthogonal and their length needs to be "1". We therefore need to find a vector that is orthogonal to the other two from 3a. A simple one is (0, 0, 1). (10 points) This is also why the third column of the rotation matrix (around the z-axis) is (0,0,1), but simple guesswork is not sufficient for full score.
